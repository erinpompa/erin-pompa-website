# Erin Pompa website — project notes

## Copy voice rule (IMPORTANT)
Use a **mix of voices**, decided by who is talking:

- **First person** — anywhere Erin speaks directly TO the visitor (hero, CTAs, offers, direct address).
  e.g. "I help you shift your perspective." / "Book me to speak."
- **Third person** — anywhere the page is INTRODUCING Erin (bio / about sections).
  e.g. "Erin Pompa is a speaker, educator, and author who…"

Rule of thumb: **Erin talking TO you → first person. The page introducing Erin → third person.**

Other standing rules from the client:
- **No em dashes** anywhere in visible copy.
- "the shift starts here" is a recurring tagline/throughline.
- Testimonials are real (Beverly Gross, Jessica Enright, Agnieszka Kajrukszto, Toni Anthony) — never fabricate reviews.
- Photography/video/logos are user-supplied; use labeled placeholders until provided.
